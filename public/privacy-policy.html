Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

01. Data Collection

We collect minimal data to provide the simulation service. This includes:

  • Phone numbers (associated with WhatsApp accounts) for identification.
  • Message content sent to the bot for processing and response generation.
  • Basic device information for analytics and debugging.

02. WhatsApp Data Usage

Your WhatsApp data is used strictly for:

  • Sending and receiving messages within the simulation context.
  • Personalizing the automated responses provided by our AI engine.

We do not use your WhatsApp data for marketing, nor do we share it with third parties.

03. Webhook & Infrastructure

Our service utilizes secure webhooks to communicate with the WhatsApp Business API.

Endpoint: /api/webhook

All data transmitted via webhooks is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+.

04. Data Storage

Primary data storage is handled locally on your device (LocalStorage) for the simulation state.

Server-side logs are ephemeral and automatically rotated. We do not maintain a permanent database of your conversation history on our servers.

05. User Rights & Deletion

You retain full control over your data.

Local Data

Clear your browser cache or use the "Reset" button in the admin panel to wipe all local simulation data instantly.

Request Deletion

For server logs, visit our Data Deletion Page to submit a formal request.
