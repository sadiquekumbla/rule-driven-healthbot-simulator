Local Data

Most simulation data sits in your browser storage. You can wipe this instantly by clearing your browser cache or clicking "Reset Simulation" in the Admin Panel.

Facebook Data

If you used Facebook Login or WhatsApp connection based on your user ID, follow these steps to remove the app:

  1. Go to your Facebook Profile > Settings & Privacy > Settings.
  2. Scroll to Apps and Websites within your settings.
  3. Find "HealthBot Simulator" and click "Remove".
